About

I feel so fortunate to exist at this time in human evolution, at a technological epoch, totally unimaginable from half a century ago. Our lives are becoming more and more digitally integrated by the day and coursing through the circuits of our cars, our computers, all of our smart devices, are billions upon billions of lines of code. Programming underpins nearly every aspect of our daily lives, and as a software developer, I think that is absolutely inspiring.I am constantly motivated by the fact that I can never know all there is to know about software development. I love the idea of a field that demands I am constantly learning new skill sets, honing the skills I already have, and never stagnating. Every day brings the gift of potential, waiting to be unwrapped by the curious, ready to inspire the world.

Projects

Perfect Pairs

A good book deserves a fitting soundtrack. Perfect Pairs is designed to suggest a variety of records to compliment what you are currently reading. Break out your bindings, curl up in your most comfortable chair and settle in with your Perfect Pair.

Mockcal

Mock-cal reverse engineers the Unix shell (Bash) cal program. I worked in collaboration with my classmate Dave Harms on this project. Written in Node.JS and testing using Chai, Mocha and Istanbul.

Reading Buddy

A Full Stack CRUD book tracking application that enables a user to search for books, add them to a wishlist, currently reading list or completed list. Built using React and Redux on the front end, Node.js on the back end and MongoDb as the database.
